ingredients:
  - 75ml vodka
  - 50ml cointreau
  - 25ml lime juice
  - 25ml cranberry juice
  - orange peel

  - sweet
  - codka
  - cointreau
  - lime
  - cranberry
---

1. Shake the vodka, cointreau, lime juice and cranberry juice with ice and strain in a chilled martini glass.
1. Garnish with an orange peel and serve.
