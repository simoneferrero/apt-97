---
title: 'Aperol Spritz'
excerpt: 'The king of aperitif.'
date: '2022-09-27T22:02:36.786Z'
author: Simone
ingredients:
  - 60ml prosecco
  - 40ml Aperol
  - 20ml soda
  - orange slice
tags:
  - sour
  - prosecco
  - aperol
  - orange
  - spritz
---

1. Pour the prosecco, aperol and soda to an ice-filled wine glass, and stir.
1. Garnish with a slice of orange and serve.
