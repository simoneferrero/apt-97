---
title: 'Tom Collins'
excerpt: ''
date: '2022-09-30T21:11:23.166Z'
author: Simone
ingredients:
  - 50ml gin
  - 25ml lemon juice
  - 15ml sugar syrup
  - soda
tags:
  - sour
  - gin
  - lemon
---

1. Shake the gin, lemon juice and syrup with ice and strain in an ice-filled collins glass.
1. Add soda, stir and serve with straws.
