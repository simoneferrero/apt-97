---
title: 'Cuba Libre'
excerpt: ''
date: '2022-09-30T21:11:23.166Z'
author: Simone
ingredients:
  - 75ml Coca Cola
  - 50ml rhum
  - 10ml lime juice
  - 2 dash Angostura
  - lime wedge
tags:
  - sweet
  - cola
  - rhum
  - lime
  - angostura
---

1. Pour cola, rhum, lime juice and angostura into a glass and stir.
1. Garnish with a lime wedge and serve.
